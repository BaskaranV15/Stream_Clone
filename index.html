Interstellar

"Interstellar" is considered a great film due to its ambitious themes, breathtaking visuals, emotional depth, and strong performances. It tackles complex scientific ideas, like relativity and black holes, while maintaining a focus on human connection, particularly the bond between a father and daughter.

Adithya's aggressive nature also earned him a reputation among his juniors as a college bully
